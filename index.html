About

The D Language Symposium is a 2-day event -- day 1 is about talking, day 2 is about action!

The D Language Symposium will be hosted at Yale University on campus in New Haven, Connecticut. Registration for the event provides you access to both the day of talks, and the hackathon.

Note: You must register to attend this event.

Yale University

Yale University is located in New Haven, Ct. USA
